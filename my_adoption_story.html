my story

I was legally adopted at about 1 year old but I started living with my adopted family for about 6 months. I have always been open about my adoption. My biological parents were both Mexican but my adoptive parents are European and Mexican. My brother, unlike me, was not adopted; he is my parents' biological child. The first photo in the slideshow to the right is a photo of me and my bio mom right before I was taken and given to a foster parent. I do not know much about my biological family. I know my mom was addicted to meth and therefore could not take care of me. She had other children prior to me and probably after me so I do have biological siblings but I do not know them.
