an adoptee

I was born on April 19 in College Station /Bryan, Texas. My birth mother was 19 years old and gave brith to my full biological sister 14 months before giving birth to me. My birth mother and father had known each other since middle school but were not in a committed relationship. My biological father was in and out of jail/prison and from my understanding my bio mother’s family wasn’t willing to help take care of another baby (me). I spent 24 days with a foster family until my parents adopted me. My biological family gave me by Native American and Mexican genetics while my parents raised me in a Turkish American culture. When I was 27 I found my biological family. Unfortunately, my bio mother’s new family doesn’t know about me and therefore my bio mom wasn’t interested in having a relationship with me. However, my bio mom told me more about my paternal side who accepted me into their family with warm arms. I learned that my father has 6 children, two of which I have met. In the picture to the right you can see my biological grandma from my father’s side and his sister (my aunt), and me.

an adoptive parent

When I married my husband my only condition was to adopt a child. After we had our first child we decided to look more into adoption. The adoption process was long and hard we waited 3 years before we had the chance to legally adopt our child.
